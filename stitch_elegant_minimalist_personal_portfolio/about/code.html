THE ARCHITECTURAL EDITORIAL

Curation is the art of intentional omission.

I am a visual storyteller and design consultant specializing in high-end architectural environments. For over a decade, I have balanced the tension between historical preservation and modernist minimalism.

My approach is rooted in the belief that a space should tell a story before a single word is spoken. It’s not just about what is present, but about the silence between the pieces.
